Frequently Asked Questions

If you have questions, these might help.

Q: Do I need an Appointment?
A: No. There is never an appointment needed. Come in and see us at whatever time is most convenient for you.

Q: How do I get paid?
A: CASH!

Q: What do you pay for Gold / Silver / Platinum / Palladium?
A: It all depends on the purity of the metal, the weight and the current spot price of the day.

Q: What if I have no idea what my Gold / Silver is worth?
A: We pride ourselves on maximizing the amount we can pay you for your Gold, Silver, Platinum, & Palladium pieces.

Q: Can I call and speak to someone before I come in to answer my question?
A: Absolutely! Call 303-282-4651. We encourage it so if we can help you with any directions etc please call us.
